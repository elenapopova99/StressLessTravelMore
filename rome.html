Instagram Worthy Places

Trevi Fountain is the most Instagrammable location that you must visit! It is located in Piazza di Trevi and stands as the largest Baroque fountain in the city. It is extremely beautiful, and even more beautiful at night-time, where it is all lit up and ancient statues are in the spotlight. You must keep with the tradition by throwing coins in the fountain and make a wish – you never know it may as well come true!

The Spanish Steps is another ‘must see’ attraction in Rome and with 138 steps combined with gorgeous curves and straight lines – they’re utterly photogenic too. The Steps are an example of Italian Baroque architecture and can be found on the Piazza di Spagna, one of Rome’s most famous squares and near Via dei Condotti, Via Frattina and Via del Babuino, which are some of the best areas for shopping.

Restaurants and Cafes

Aroma is the best restaurant to have the perfect Colosseum view for a photo. The Michelin-starred restaurant serves the most fantastic food too!

Il Giardino at Hotel Eden is a gorgeous bar where you can get views of the Eternal City stretching from the Il Vittoriano and St Peters to the Villa Medici where the Borghese gardens in the background can be captured in your photos.
